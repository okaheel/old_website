My work

Projects:

NCAR (July 2019 - Present)

As part of the Earth Observing Lab(EOL) at the National Center for Atmospheric Research(NCAR), Working under senior software engineers, I am contributing to on-going improvement of search and discovery of data sets through ontology development, and a planned   proof of concept project to classify and categorize metadata utilizing machine learning frameworks. Additionally I am supporting the cataloging of data and the verification of metadata, including the processing, quality control, and archiving of meteorological data sets from EOL supported field programs using data conversion and management software.

CAIRO Lab (May 2018 - May 2019)

The Collaborative Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Lab(CAIRO) is a lab based in the Computer Science department at CU Boulder, which focuses on algorithms that enable artificially intelligent agents and robots to safely and effectively collaborate with humans. I have been working on an independent study project that was centered on creating a machine learning algorithm that is able to pick out “good” portraits out of a test set using tensorflow, keras, and other tools.
